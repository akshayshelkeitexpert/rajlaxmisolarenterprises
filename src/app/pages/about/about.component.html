Who we are

India’s trusted solar BOS & module distributor — with a full-service consumer electronics shop under one roof.

SOLAR SYSTEM LLP

Rajlaxmi Solar Enterprises — authorised distribution you can rely on

Solar System LLP operates as Rajlaxmi Solar Enterprises. We are an authorised channel partner of WAAREE, WAACAB, Lauritz Knudsen Electrical & Automation (L&T), Elmex, and FiberX. We keep industry-leading solar SKUs in stock across multiple warehouses so integrators and installers get fast delivery at competitive prices.

Rajlaxmi Solar Enterprises is a pure-play solar distribution business focused on panels, inverters, cables, ACDB/DCDB, meters, MC4 & harnesses, FRP walkways, Li-ion storage, and complete project kits — including solutions aligned with Surya Ghar Muft Bijli Yojana.

Rajlaxmi Mobile & Electronics is our secondary retail line at the same location: mobiles, TVs, earphones, refrigerators, washing machines, and more — so customers can shop consumer tech while we stay laser-focused on solar as our primary business.
