Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers on how we supply solar material, support projects, and work with rooftop owners and EPCs. Call +91 94036 11664 for anything specific.

1. Are you an authorised distributor?

Yes. Rajlaxmi Solar Enterprises (Solar System LLP) is an authorised channel partner for brands including WAAREE, WAACAB, L&T (Lauritz Knudsen), Elmex, and FiberX — ask for current certificates when you place a PO.

2. Do you stock DCR modules for subsidy projects?

We carry DCR and non-DCR lines depending on programme rules. Share your state scheme, capacity, and timeline — we’ll match SKU and documentation (datasheets, warranty, compliance notes) to your application.

3. Can you supply a full bill of materials?

Yes — modules, inverter, DC/AC cable, MC4 and harnesses, ACDB/DCDB, SPDs, and net/gen meters in one go. That reduces site delays and mismatched ratings between inverter strings and DBs.

4. What about Surya Ghar or DISCOM-specific meters?

We stock net and generation meters suited to common DISCOM specs, including single- and three-phase models with built-in CT options where required. Confirm your utility’s approved list before ordering.

5. Is Rajlaxmi Mobile & Electronics part of the same business?

Yes — Rajlaxmi Mobile & Electronics is our consumer retail (phones, TVs, appliances). Solar distribution remains our primary focus; the shop is secondary and convenient for customers who already trust us.
