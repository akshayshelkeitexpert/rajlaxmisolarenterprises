Last updated: April 2026. This policy describes our practices for this website and routine business contact.

1. Information we collect

We may collect information that you voluntarily provide, such as:

  • Name, phone number, email address, and organisation when you call, message, email, or submit enquiry forms.
  • Project or product details you share for quotations, BOQs, or technical support.

We do not intentionally collect sensitive personal data unless you choose to send it to us for a legitimate business purpose.

2. How we use information

We use this information to respond to enquiries, prepare quotes, fulfil orders, comply with legal and tax requirements, and improve our service. We do not sell your personal data to third parties for marketing.

3. Sharing

We may share limited information with logistics partners, payment processors, or manufacturers where needed to deliver products or honour warranties. We may also disclose information if required by law or to protect our legal rights.

4. Cookies and analytics

This site may use basic cookies or similar technologies needed for the site to function. If we add analytics or marketing tools later, we will update this policy and, where required, seek appropriate consent.

5. Data retention

We retain business records as needed for accounting, tax, and warranty support, and in line with applicable law. Enquiry details are kept only as long as necessary for those purposes.

6. Your choices

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal information where applicable law allows. Contact us using the details on our Contact page. We will respond within a reasonable time.

7. Security

We take reasonable steps to protect information we hold. No online transmission is completely secure; please avoid sharing confidential credentials by unsecured channels.
