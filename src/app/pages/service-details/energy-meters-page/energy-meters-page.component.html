We supply net meters and generation meters for rooftop and C&I — single-phase 2-wire and three-phase 4-wire, with accuracies typically class 1.0 where specified. Measurements commonly include kWh, V, A, kVAh, kVArh; some models add TOD and quadrant metering for programmes that require it.

CT-operated and built-in CT options cover higher currents; ABT and utility-listed variants are available for approved lists — always confirm your DISCOM approved model list before ordering.

Ideal companions to grid-tie inverters and rooftop systems under Surya Ghar and state net-metering rules.

Core highlights

  • Types: Net metering and generation (export) meters.
  • Phases: Single-phase 2-wire and three-phase 4-wire.
  • Accuracy: Typically class 1.0 where specified.
  • Options: CT-operated and built-in CT; TOD / quadrant on select models.

Best suited for

  • Homes and businesses completing net-metering paperwork.
  • Installers who need the utility-approved SKU for your circle.
  • Surya Ghar and state rooftop programmes with defined meter rules.
  • Upgrades when the DISCOM changes the approved meter list.
